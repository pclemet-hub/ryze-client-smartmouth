## B2B / Professional Channel

- **URL:** partners.smartmouth.com
- **Products:** Clinical DDS Oral Rinse, bulk single packs, professional samples
- **Audience:** Dentists, hygienists, dental school programs
- **Value Prop:** Clinically proven outcomes they can recommend to patients

## Where-to-Buy Locator

SmartMouth operates a dedicated store locator: `smartmouth.com/where-to-buy/`
Drives consumers to nearest physical retail — omnichannel strategy.

## Price Points by Channel

**DTC (smartmouth.com):**
- Full MSRP, full selection
- Bundles and multi-packs available

**Amazon:**
- MSRP-aligned or slight premium (FBA/Prime)
- Subscribe & Save likely available
- Reviews are key social proof driver here

**Retail (Walgreens/CVS/Target):**
- MSRP, curated selection (not all SKUs)
- Shelf placement in oral care aisle
