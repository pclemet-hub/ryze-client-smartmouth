# SmartMouth Full Brand Research Profile

## Executive Summary

SmartMouth is a science-driven oral care brand specializing in bad breath elimination and prevention using patented zinc ion technology. Positioned as the clinically-proven alternative to traditional mouthwashes, SmartMouth competes in the premium segment with a focus on efficacy over masking symptoms.

**Established:** 1993
**Parent Company:** Triumph Pharmaceuticals Inc. (SmartMouth Oral Health Laboratories)
**Headquarters:** Saint Louis, Missouri
**Current President & CEO:** Mark (joined September 2024, former P&G/PepsiCo executive)
**Press contact:** marketing@smartmouth.com | 314-995-3090

---

## Founding Story

- **Founded by:** Dr. Marvin Cohen, leading halitosis authority
  - Author of the bad breath (halitosis) section of Encyclopedia Britannica (1998 Medical Edition)
  - Recognized market needed root-cause solutions, not masking
- **Core technology developed by:** Dr. Israel Kleinberg, Chairman of Oral Biology and Pathology, SUNY Stony Brook
- **Collaboration:** Cohen's halitosis expertise + Kleinberg's research = zinc ion activation technology

## Scientific Backing

### Clinical Study
- **Institutions:** Saint Louis University Center for Advanced Dental Education + Southern Illinois University
- **Publication:** *Oral Health and Preventive Dentistry* (peer-reviewed)
- **Result:** SmartMouth Clinical DDS outperformed chlorhexidine on:
  - Tooth staining (less)
  - Taste preference (better)
  - Patient compliance (higher)

### Safety Profile
- All ingredients FDA-approved as GRAS (Generally Recognized As Safe)
- Alcohol-free across all formulations
- Targeted mechanism (no broad antimicrobial side effects)
