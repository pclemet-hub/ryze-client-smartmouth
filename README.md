# SmartMouth — Design System Context

**Client:** SmartMouth Oral Health Laboratories (Triumph Pharmaceuticals Inc.)
**Website:** https://smartmouth.com/
**Industry:** Premium Oral Care / Health & Wellness
**Location:** Saint Louis, Missouri
**Founded:** 1993

> SmartMouth is a science-driven oral care brand specializing in bad breath elimination and prevention using patented zinc ion technology. Positioned as the clinically-proven alternative to traditional mouthwashes.

---

## Quick Reference for Claude Design

**Primary Brand Claim:** "Never Have Bad Breath" — The only mouthwash clinically proven to eliminate & prevent bad breath for 24 hours with just 2 rinses a day.

**Design Personality:** Clinical precision + approachable confidence. Clean, white-dominant with green and blue activation accents. Scientific without being cold.

**Core Colors:**
- Activation Green: `#4CAF50` (freshly mixed mouthwash = product is working)
- Efficacy Blue: `#2196F3` (post-activation, visual feedback system)
- Clinical White: `#FFFFFF`
- Text: `#212121`

**Typeface Direction:** Modern sans-serif (Inter, DM Sans, or Helvetica Neue). Clean and direct. Monospace only for clinical/data callouts.
