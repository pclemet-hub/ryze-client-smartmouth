# Brand Positioning

## Core Positioning Statement

> "SmartMouth is the scientifically-proven mouthwash that eliminates bad breath at the source — not just the symptom — giving health-conscious consumers the confidence to live their fullest life."

## Competitive Frame

SmartMouth competes in the **premium oral care segment**, positioned against:
- **Mainstream**: Listerine, Colgate, Crest, ACT
- **Premium natural**: Tom's of Maine, TheraBreath
- **Dental professional**: Chlorhexidine prescription rinses

## Key Differentiators

| Differentiator | Claim |
|---------------|-------|
| Mechanism | Zinc ions block sulfur gas production at source (not masking) |
| Duration | 24-hour proven protection with 2 rinses/day |
| Technology | Patented dual-chamber bottle — fresh activation every time |
| Clinical backing | Peer-reviewed journals, outperformed chlorhexidine in clinical trials |
| History | 30+ years since 1993 — not a trend brand |
| Range | Specific formulas per condition: dry mouth, gum health, mouth sores |

## Brand History

- **1993** — Founded in Saint Louis, MO by Dr. Marvin Cohen (leading halitosis authority, Encyclopedia Britannica contributor)
- **Technology** — Zinc ion activation developed by Dr. Israel Kleinberg, SUNY Stony Brook
- **Entity** — Triumph Pharmaceuticals Inc. dba SmartMouth Oral Health Laboratories
- **Current CEO** — Mark (joined Sept 2024, background: P&G / PepsiCo)
- **Board Chair** — Ray Barrett

## Target Audience

### Primary: Health-Conscious Problem Solvers
- Ages 30–60
- Higher income (premium price tolerance)
- Trust science over marketing
- Seek solutions, not band-aids
- Bad breath is a recurring, embarrassing problem for them

### Secondary: Dental Professional Referrals
- Dentist-recommended to patients
- Clinical DDS line for diagnosed gum disease / gingivitis
- B2B portal: partners.smartmouth.com

### Tertiary: Condition-Specific Buyers
- Dry mouth sufferers (medication side effects, aging)
- Gum disease patients
- Mouth sore recovery
- Post-surgery oral care

## Emotional Driver

SmartMouth isn't selling mouthwash. It's selling **confidence in close-quarters moments** — job interviews, first dates, important conversations, time with family. The product removes a source of anxiety and shame that many people deal with silently.

## Visual Representation of Audience

**Not**: Fear-based imagery, embarrassed people, comedic bad-breath tropes
**Yes**: Confident people in professional/personal moments, clinical/scientific imagery, real testimonials from real people
