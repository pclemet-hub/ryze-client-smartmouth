# SmartMouth Tone of Voice

## Brand Personality

**Professional Yet Approachable** — Scientific credibility without jargon.
Think: a confident dentist who also happens to be your friend.

| Trait | In Practice |
|-------|-------------|
| **Direct** | State the problem, state the fix. No hedging. |
| **Confident** | "The ONLY mouthwash clinically proven…" — they own their claim |
| **Educational** | Explains VSCs, zinc ions, sulfur — but accessibly |
| **Empowering** | Focus on confidence gained, not embarrassment avoided |
| **Trustworthy** | 30+ years of research. Backed by dentists. Peer-reviewed. |

## Messaging Hierarchy

### Tier 1 — Primary Claim (always lead with this)
- **"Never Have Bad Breath"**
- "The only mouthwash clinically proven to eliminate & prevent bad breath for 24 hours"

### Tier 2 — How It Works (the proof)
- "Patented zinc ion activated technology"
- "Blocks bacteria from producing sulfur gas at the source"
- "Dual-chamber bottle — fresh activation with every rinse"
- "Just 2 rinses a day. That's it."

### Tier 3 — Why It Matters (the benefit)
- "Addresses root cause, not symptoms"
- "30+ years of research and innovation"
- "Recommended by dental professionals nationwide"

### Tier 4 — Reassurance (remove objections)
- "All ingredients FDA-approved as Generally Recognized As Safe"
- "60-day money-back satisfaction guarantee"
- "Gentle, alcohol-free formula"
- "Clinically proven in peer-reviewed journals"

## Voice Do's & Don'ts

### Do
- Lead with the outcome ("bad breath eliminated")
- Use confidence language ("the only," "proven," "guaranteed")
- Reference the science briefly without going deep
- Show the simple daily routine (2 rinses = 24 hours)
- Acknowledge the emotional weight of bad breath — confidence, relationships, career

### Don't
- Fear-based or shame-based language ("Don't be that person with bad breath")
- Jargon without explanation (say "zinc ions block bacteria" not "ZnCl₂ inhibits VSC production")
- Overpromise cosmetically (whitening, etc.) — that's not their lane
- Downplay competitors by name — lead with superiority, not attacks
